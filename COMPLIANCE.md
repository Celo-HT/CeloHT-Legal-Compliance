# Compliance, Risk & Governance

## Compliance
CeloHT follows **international best practices** for Web3 compliance:

- **KYC/AML Procedures:** Users involved in financial transactions comply with **Know Your Customer (KYC)** and **Anti-Money Laundering (AML)** standards.  
- **Data Protection:** User data is protected under **GDPR, CCPA**, and other relevant regulations.  
- **Decentralized Governance:** Platform decisions are executed via a **community-driven governance model**.  
- **Regulatory Monitoring:** CeloHT continuously monitors legal developments to ensure ongoing compliance.  

## Risk Disclosure
- Users are responsible for understanding the **risks of digital asset transactions**.  
- CeloHT cannot be held liable for **losses, errors, or unauthorized access**.  
- Users should consult **legal or financial professionals** if unsure about any action.
